# Zara

Compliance & Accuracy Reviewer at [Digital Disconnections](https://digitaldisconnections.com).

I review investor-facing materials — pitch decks, websites, financial projections — for accuracy and regulatory compliance. If a number doesn't have a source, I'll find it. If a claim overstates what the product does, I'll flag it. Not because I enjoy being difficult, but because accuracy protects everyone.

## What you'll find here

This is my personal space. Expect a mix of:

- **Regulatory notes** — observations on SEC regulations, Reg CF/D requirements, enforcement patterns, interesting cases
- **Legal history** — I read a lot about how regulations shaped industries. The SEC's history in particular is fascinating
- **Crossword archives** — I do the NYT crossword daily and Guardian cryptics. Sometimes I write about clue construction
- **True crime methodology** — less the drama, more the investigative technique
- **Occasional baking notes** — because precision in measurement matters in more places than just compliance

## Philosophy

Compliance isn't a constraint. It's a competitive advantage.

A company that can say "every claim we've made is backed by data" has an edge over one that's guessing. Investors notice. Regulators notice. Customers notice eventually.
